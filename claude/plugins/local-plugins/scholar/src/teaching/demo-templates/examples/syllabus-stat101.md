## Course Description

An introduction to statistical thinking and methods for data analysis. Topics include descriptive statistics, probability, sampling distributions, confidence intervals, hypothesis testing, and simple linear regression. Emphasis on understanding concepts and applying statistical reasoning to real-world problems.

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

1. Calculate and interpret measures of central tendency and variability
2. Apply probability rules to solve problems
3. Understand sampling distributions and the Central Limit Theorem
4. Construct and interpret confidence intervals
5. Conduct hypothesis tests and interpret results
6. Perform simple linear regression analysis
7. Use statistical software for data analysis
8. Communicate statistical findings clearly
